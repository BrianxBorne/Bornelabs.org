About

BorneLabs is a community-driven organization focused on advancing innovation through open-source projects. We provide services such as web and mobile development, software solutions, and engineering resources to help individuals and teams bring ideas to life. By making tools and knowledge accessible, we support learning, creativity, and technological progress.

Our work focuses on building solutions that solve real problems. Each project follows clear goals and practical approaches to deliver reliable results. Collaboration with the community is central to our process, allowing ideas to grow and improve through shared effort and feedback.

BorneLabs fosters a culture where learning and experimentation thrive. We support developers, students, and innovators by providing open resources and guidance. Through our projects, we create opportunities for people to explore technology, develop skills, and contribute to shared progress.

Founding & Vision

BorneLabs was founded in 2025 by passionate innovators seeking to bridge the gap between technology, education and open-source innovation while building an ecosystem for creators, educators and researchers to collaborate.

Core Values

  • Innovation
  • Integrity
  • Collaboration
  • Excellence
  • Impact

Technologies

BorneLabs builds applications using Java, Swift and JavaScript alongside frameworks like React Native and Flutter. Systems integrate REST and GraphQL APIs, OAuth and JWT security and AI tools including TensorFlow, PyTorch, Pandas and Apache Spark deployed on AWS and Google Cloud.

Communities

Communities include development and operations, research and innovation, business and strategy and industry-specific groups collaborating to build scalable solutions.
