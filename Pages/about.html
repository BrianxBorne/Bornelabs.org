About

BorneLabs is a community-driven hub for technological and creative innovation. Our mission is to inspire and empower developers, innovators, and enthusiasts by providing tools, resources, and opportunities for collaboration.

Software and Solutions Wing: Specializing in general software development including applications, games and integrating AI technologies to deliver practical and transformative solutions.

Innovation and Exploration Wing: A platform for showcasing and sharing groundbreaking innovations across diverse fields while providing accessible blueprints and resources that fuel community-driven advancements.

Education and Outreach Wing: Focused on fostering growth and learning through workshops, webinars, and hackathons while providing open-source educational materials to inspire the next generation of innovators.

Additional Information

Founding & Vision

BorneLabs was founded in 2025 by passionate innovators seeking to bridge the gap between technology, education and open-source innovation while building an ecosystem for creators, educators and researchers to collaborate.

Core Values

  • Innovation
  • Integrity
  • Collaboration
  • Excellence
  • Impact

Technologies

BorneLabs builds applications using Java, Swift and JavaScript alongside frameworks like React Native and Flutter. Systems integrate REST and GraphQL APIs, OAuth and JWT security and AI tools including TensorFlow, PyTorch, Pandas and Apache Spark deployed on AWS and Google Cloud.

Communities

Communities include development and operations, research and innovation, business and strategy and industry-specific groups collaborating to build scalable solutions.
